Welcome to CRIS Github Page

The Centre for Robotics & Intelligent Systems (CRIS), formerly the Mobile & Marine Robotics Research Centre (MMRRC), is the only research centre in Ireland dedicated to robotic applications and the development of marine robotics on the island. Established in 2000 at the University of Limerick, CRIS brings together a highly capable engineering group composed of postdoctoral researchers and PhD students from diverse disciplines including electronic, computer, mechanical, and aeronautical engineering.

The group focuses on practical, industrially relevant marine technologies. From marine platforms to navigation systems, sensor development, emergency response planning, remote operated vehicle (ROV) technologies, and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), we are actively engaged in building a wide range of robotic systems.

CRIS is an integral part of MaREI, the SFI Centre for Marine & Renewable Energy, and contributes to national, European, and industry-collaborative projects.
